About Us

Khalet El Ayal is an Egyptian company founded in 2021 to support mothers in caring for their children with a unique approach that blends professionalism and warmth. We started with a simple idea: providing "in-home babysitting" services to help working mothers save time and ensure their children are well cared for.

Our goal is for children to feel as if they are with a family member, which is why we call our babysitters Khalat. We don’t just focus on babysitting; we aim to develop children’s skills using proven methods like the Montessori approach, which encourages learning through play and exploration.

What We Do

Expert Sitters

The babysitters at Khalet El Ayal are highly trained and experienced. We carefully select qualified sitters to ensure safe and enjoyable care for children, with continuous follow-ups to improve performance and provide peace of mind for parents.

Montosory Curriculum

The Montessori method focuses on fostering independence through self-directed learning and interaction with the environment. It encourages exploration and development in a flexible, hands-on setting tailored to each child's needs.
